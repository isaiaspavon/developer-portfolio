I'm currently studying Computer Science at the University of Georgia. My focus areas include full-stack development, mobile software, and AI integration.

During my time at UGA, I've worked on a variety of impactful projects — from building Android apps with Firebase backends to leading hackathon teams that tackled real-world challenges with AI. I take pride in writing clean, scalable code that solves real problems.

This summer, I will intern at GMS where I will focus on backend development, and contribution to the modernization of legacy systems. I will work closely with the engineering team to refactor and transition key modules from on-premise environments to AWS cloud infrastructure. Using technologies like Progress 4GL (OpenEdge ABL), SQL, and RESTful APIs, I will help streamline internal processes and improve system reliability. Whether I’m collaborating with teammates, debugging late into the night, or presenting a demo, I approach every challenge with curiosity and grit. I’m always ready to learn, adapt, and push boundaries — because building cool stuff is what I love to do.
